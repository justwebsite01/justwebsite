Four Decades of Dedicated Practice

Adv. Ashok Dhende has been at the forefront of legal practice in Pune since 1984 — building Dhende Associates into a full-service firm known for its depth of knowledge, client-centric approach, and unwavering professional ethics.

With special expertise in Civil and Criminal litigation, Motor Accident Claims, and Insurance disputes, Adv. Ashok Dhende has represented thousands of clients before Pune District Courts, and specialised tribunals across Maharashtra as well as the Bombay High Court.

Authorised Notary Public

Notarial Services

Adv. Ashok Dhende has served as a Notary Public Government of India appointed under the Notaries Act, 1952. Notarial services are available for individuals, businesses, banks, and institutions across Pune and Maharashtra.

  • Attestation of affidavits, declarations, and sworn statements
  • Authentication of documents for use abroad (apostille-ready)
  • Notarisation of power of attorney — general and special
  • Verification of copies, certificates, and academic documents
  • Official Translation of documents from English, Hindi, Marathi
  • Execution and notarisation of agreements and contracts
  • Notarial certificates for property and commercial transactions
  • Attestation of wills and testamentary documents
  • Documents for NRI matters and overseas submission
