India's Big Rulebook?

👆 TAP ME!

🏫 India's School Rulebook!

Just like your school has rules — "no bullying, be kind" — India has rules for EVERYBODY, even the Government! That rulebook is the Constitution!

🎯 If the government breaks rules, the Constitution says they must be stopped!
🔒
🗓️

When was it born?

👆 TAP ME!

🎉 26 January 1950!

On this day India became a Republic — meaning the PEOPLE are the real bosses! The Constitution gave power to every citizen!

🥳 That's why we celebrate Republic Day every year with the big parade in Delhi!
🔒
🏆

A World Record?

👆 TAP ME!

🌍 #1 in the WORLD!

India's is the longest written constitution in the world — 448 articles, 25 parts, 12 schedules!

✍️ 299 brilliant people worked for 2 yrs, 11 months & 18 days to write it!
🔒
🧑‍⚖️

Who wrote it?

👆 TAP ME!

👨‍⚖️ Dr. B.R. Ambedkar!

He was a brilliant scholar who led the team that wrote the Constitution. He fought for the rights of poor people and became India's first Law Minister!

🌟 He is called the "Father of the Indian Constitution!"

🏛️ India's 3 Power Teams!
Three teams run India — they balance each other so nobody gets too powerful!
LAW MAKERS
🏛️

Legislature

Parliament = Lok Sabha + Rajya Sabha. Like India's student council — elected by YOU to make new laws! 550+ members from all over India! 🗳️

THE DOERS
⚙️

Executive

Prime Minister + Cabinet + Officers. Like the school principal — they run India day-to-day and carry out all the laws! 🏃

THE JUDGES
⚖️

Judiciary

Supreme Court + High Courts. Like the referee in a football match — they make sure EVERYONE follows the rules, even the government! 🧑‍⚖️

🔁
These three ALWAYS check and balance each other — no single person can become too powerful!
This genius idea is called "Separation of Powers"!
📅 The Constitution's Big Journey!
From a dream to the world's biggest rulebook — in just 3 years!
🗳️
1946 — The Dream Team Forms!

🏛️ 299 brilliant people from all over India came together with ONE goal: to write the rulebook for our brand-new country!

🎆
15 Aug 1947 — FREEDOM!

🇮🇳 India became FREE from British rule! Now it was time to write OUR OWN rules — no more following someone else's laws!

✍️
1948–49 — Writing Time!

📝 Dr. B.R. Ambedkar led the Drafting Committee. Long debates, thousands of ideas, nearly 3 whole years of hard work!

✅
26 Nov 1949 — Adopted!

🎊 The Constitution was officially adopted! This is now celebrated as Constitution Day every 26th November!

🎉
26 Jan 1950 — REPUBLIC DAY! 🎊

🥁 The Constitution came to LIFE! India became a Republic — the PEOPLE became the real rulers! We celebrate this with the big parade in New Delhi every year!

👨‍⚖️

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar — Father of the Constitution!

Dr. Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar was one of India's greatest scholars. He studied at the best universities in the world. Even though people discriminated against him, he rose up and became the architect of the Constitution — giving EVERY Indian equal rights, justice, and dignity! 🌟

✏️ "The Constitution is not a mere lawyers' document. It is a vehicle of Life, and its spirit is always the spirit of Age." — Dr. B.R. Ambedkar
